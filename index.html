Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2025 | Last Updated: May 17, 2025

At NovaMind ("we," "our," or "us"), your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our AI platform, website, and services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: When you register, we collect your name, email address, billing information, and company details. Usage Data: Automatically collected information such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and API request patterns. AI Interaction Data: Prompts and responses are processed in real-time and not stored unless you explicitly opt-in for model improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide and improve our services, process payments, personalize your experience, send important updates (security, policy changes), and comply with legal obligations. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Retention & Security

We retain personal data only as long as necessary. Industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3, AES-256) protects data in transit and at rest. Access is strictly limited to authorized personnel with regular security audits.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted partners for cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP), payment processing (Stripe), and analytics (Plausible — privacy-friendly). All partners comply with GDPR/CCPA requirements.

5. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, as well as data portability. To exercise rights, email privacy@novamind.ai. You may also opt out of marketing communications at any time.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication and functional purposes. No invasive tracking. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you become aware, please contact us.

8. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in the US, EU, or other jurisdictions. We implement Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure adequate protection.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be notified via email or prominent notice on our website.

Contact Us: For privacy-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@novamind.ai or mail: NovaMind Privacy, 42 AI Avenue, San Francisco, CA 94105.

NovaMind is committed to transparent and ethical AI.
