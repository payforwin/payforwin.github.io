Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025 | Lumina AI is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy.

1. Information we collect

We collect account information (name, email), conversation history (stored locally and encrypted), usage analytics. You have full control over your data.

2. How we use your information

To provide AI assistance, improve response quality, personalize your experience, and process payments. We do NOT train models on your private conversations without explicit permission.

3. Data retention & deletion

You can delete your entire chat history at any time. Account data retained until you request deletion. Export available upon request.

4. Third-party sharing

We use secure infrastructure providers (AWS, OpenAI API). No personal data is sold or shared for advertising purposes.

5. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability. Email privacy@lumina.ai. You may also opt out of any data collection beyond essential service.

6. Security measures

Encryption at rest and in transit, regular security audits, and strict access controls.

7. Children's privacy

Lumina is intended for users 16+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. International transfers

Data may be processed globally with appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses).

9. Policy updates & contact

Material changes will be communicated via email. Data Protection Officer: dpo@lumina.ai. Address: 101 Creative Loop, Portland, OR 97204.
